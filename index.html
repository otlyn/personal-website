Hi! My name is Otlyn Black. I’m a Creative Developer specializing in multimedia. I have a decade of experience working within the intricacies of film, photography, design, and web development. Regardless of the medium, I enjoy creating things that look nice and work well.

Additionally, I’m a certified wilderness and canoe guide and enjoy swinging an axe. The picture above is from a recent cross-country skiing expedition in (my second country) Sweden, in a subarctic province called Jokkmokk. Pratar du svenska?

Besides this, I’m passionate about good conversation, bitter chocolate, philosophy and nature. I prefer aimless aimful wandering, campfire smoke and stargazing over Game of Thrones (GoT), but just barely.

I live a simple and authentic life. Integrity is very important to me. If I make a mistake, I’ll admit it. If there’s something beyond my abilities, I’ll tell you. And if the chocolate is missing, well… just assume it was me. Sorry.
